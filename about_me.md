## Why Continuous Delivery Matters for Agile Teams

Continuous Delivery (CD) is essential for agile teams because it enables fast, reliable, and repeatable releases. It ensures that new features and bug fixes can be delivered to users quickly without introducing instability.

CD improves:

- **Feedback cycles**: Faster releases mean quicker user and stakeholder feedback
- **Quality**: Automated testing and deployment reduce human error
- **Team efficiency**: Developers spend less time on manual release processes
- **Risk management**: Smaller, incremental changes are easier to test and roll back
